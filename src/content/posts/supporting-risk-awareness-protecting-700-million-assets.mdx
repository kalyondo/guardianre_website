In today's dynamic business environment, organisations face increasing uncertainty driven by economic shifts, climate risks, and operational complexities. As a result, effective risk management has become essential for long-term sustainability and resilience.

## The "Are You Covered?" Initiative

Through its *"Are You Covered?"* brand awareness initiative, Guardian Reinsurance Brokers Limited continues to highlight the importance of comprehensive risk protection for businesses operating in Zambia and across the region.

This campaign serves as a reminder that adequate insurance and reinsurance coverage is not merely a regulatory requirement but a strategic imperative for business continuity.

## A Milestone Achievement

As part of this journey, Guardian Re is proud to have contributed to the protection of **over USD 700 million (approximately K19.6 billion) in assets**, reflecting the trust placed in the firm by clients, insurers, and reinsurance partners.

This milestone underscores Guardian Re's role as a reliable reinsurance intermediary, providing tailored solutions that strengthen insurers' underwriting capacity and support businesses in safeguarding critical assets.

### What This Means for Our Clients

- **Enhanced Protection**: Access to comprehensive Assets All Risk coverage that protects against unforeseen physical loss, destruction, or damage
- **Balance Sheet Strength**: Reinsurance solutions that enable insurers to underwrite larger and more complex risks
- **Expert Guidance**: Professional advisory services backed by over 70 years of combined industry experience through our strategic partnership with Minerva Re

## Our Commitment

Guardian Re remains committed to advancing risk awareness and delivering professional reinsurance brokerage services that protect what matters most. As Zambia's first licensed reinsurance broker, we continue to uphold the values of **Excellence, Reliability, Innovativeness, and Teamwork** (ERIT) in everything we do.
