As the year draws to a close, Guardian Reinsurance Brokers Limited extends its sincere appreciation to its clients, partners, and stakeholders for the trust and collaboration that have defined 2025.

## Reflecting on Our Journey

This year has been marked by significant achievements, meaningful engagements, and continued growth. From protecting over **USD 700 million in assets** to participating in landmark industry conferences across the continent, Guardian Re has remained committed to delivering excellence in reinsurance brokerage.

### Highlights of 2025

- **51st AIO Conference**: Engaging with continental leaders in Addis Ababa on the future of African insurance
- **IAZ Conference 2025**: Exploring innovation and resilience in Zambia's evolving insurance landscape
- **Insurance Week Zambia**: Celebrating the sector's growth and discussing community resilience
- **Continued Partnership with Minerva Re**: Strengthening our access to global markets and specialist resources

## The Power of Partnership

The firm's achievements throughout the year have been made possible through strong partnerships, shared objectives, and a collective commitment to excellence.

Guardian Re values the relationships built with:

- **Our Clients**: The insurers and cedants who trust us to structure and place their reinsurance programmes
- **Reinsurance Partners**: The global and regional reinsurers who provide the capacity that underpins our placements
- **Minerva Re**: Our strategic partner whose expertise and resources enhance our service delivery
- **Regulatory Authorities**: The PIA and other bodies whose guidance ensures market integrity

## Looking Ahead to 2026

As we look forward to the new year, Guardian Re remains committed to:

- Delivering reliable and professional reinsurance solutions
- Supporting our clients' evolving risk management needs
- Contributing to the development of Zambia's insurance industry
- Upholding our ERIT values of Excellence, Reliability, Innovativeness, and Teamwork

## Season's Greetings

Guardian Re wishes its clients, partners, and their families a joyful holiday season and a prosperous New Year.

May 2026 bring continued success, good health, and new opportunities for growth.
