### About Guardian Re

Guardian Reinsurance Brokers (Z) Limited is Zambia's first licensed reinsurance broker, established in 2006. We provide professional reinsurance broking and advisory services to the African market in association with **Minerva Re**.

Our team brings over **70 years of combined experience** through strategic partnerships, offering access to global reinsurance markets, specialist technical resources, and advanced analytical capabilities.

We are licensed and regulated by the **Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA)** of Zambia.
