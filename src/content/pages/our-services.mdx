## Reinsurance Broking and Advisory Services

Guardian Re provides comprehensive reinsurance broking and advisory services to insurance companies operating in Zambia and across the African continent. We have evolved from traditional placement services to offer fully integrated reinsurance advisory and broking solutions designed to support our clients' strategic objectives.

Our service offering spans twelve distinct areas of expertise, each delivered by experienced professionals with deep technical knowledge and market insight.

---

### 1. Reinsurance Structuring

We design bespoke reinsurance programmes tailored to the specific risk profile, capital position, and strategic priorities of each client. Our structuring process considers treaty and facultative options, quota share and excess of loss arrangements, and optimal retention levels.

**Key deliverables:**
- Programme design and optimisation
- Retention analysis and recommendations
- Treaty wording review and development
- Capacity allocation strategies

---

### 2. Negotiation and Placement

Our experienced broking team negotiates with reinsurers on behalf of our clients to secure favourable terms, competitive pricing, and appropriate coverage. We leverage our market relationships and technical credibility to achieve optimal placement outcomes.

**Key deliverables:**
- Market presentation and submission
- Terms negotiation and benchmarking
- Placement coordination across multiple markets
- Contract finalisation and documentation

---

### 3. Reinsurance Security Analysis

We conduct rigorous assessments of reinsurer financial strength, creditworthiness, and claims-paying ability. Our security analysis helps clients make informed decisions about counterparty selection and portfolio diversification.

**Key deliverables:**
- Credit rating analysis and monitoring
- Financial strength assessment
- Counterparty concentration review
- Security panel recommendations

---

### 4. Catastrophe Management

Guardian Re provides expert guidance on catastrophic risk exposures, including natural disasters, large-loss events, and accumulation scenarios. We assist clients in quantifying and managing their catastrophe risk through appropriate reinsurance solutions.

**Key deliverables:**
- Catastrophe exposure assessment
- Modelling and scenario analysis
- Event response coordination
- Loss estimation and notification

---

### 5. Analytics, Products and Solutions

Our analytical capabilities enable data-driven decision-making across all aspects of reinsurance programme management. We provide actuarial analysis, portfolio modelling, and performance metrics to support strategic planning.

**Key deliverables:**
- Portfolio performance analysis
- Loss experience studies
- Pricing support and validation
- Product development assistance

---

### 6. Reinsurance and Portfolio Optimisation

We assist clients in optimising their reinsurance arrangements to achieve the most efficient use of capital while maintaining appropriate risk protection. Our optimisation approach balances cost, coverage, and capital efficiency.

**Key deliverables:**
- Capital efficiency analysis
- Programme cost-benefit assessment
- Portfolio rebalancing recommendations
- Risk-adjusted return analysis

---

### 7. Peer Analysis and Benchmarking

Guardian Re provides competitive intelligence and benchmarking services to help clients understand their market position relative to peers. Our analysis covers programme structures, pricing levels, and market practices.

**Key deliverables:**
- Peer comparison studies
- Market practice benchmarking
- Performance metric analysis
- Competitive positioning insights

---

### 8. Rating Advisory

We provide expert guidance to insurers seeking to maintain or improve their credit ratings. Our advisory services cover the reinsurance-related aspects of rating agency requirements and expectations.

**Key deliverables:**
- Rating agency liaison support
- Reinsurance programme assessment
- Documentation and presentation preparation
- Ongoing monitoring and advice

---

### 9. Life and Health Reinsurance

Our Life and Health team provides specialised broking services for life insurers, health insurers, and pension schemes. We address the unique requirements of long-term and health-related risks.

**Key deliverables:**
- Life treaty placement
- Health reinsurance solutions
- Mortality and morbidity risk transfer
- Product development support

---

### 10. Market Intelligence

Guardian Re maintains close relationships with global reinsurance markets, enabling us to provide clients with timely and relevant market intelligence. Our insights support informed decision-making throughout the reinsurance cycle.

**Key deliverables:**
- Market trend analysis
- Capacity and pricing updates
- Regulatory development monitoring
- Strategic market insights

---

### 11. Policy Administration

We provide comprehensive administrative support for the ongoing management of reinsurance contracts. Our administration services ensure accurate documentation, timely reporting, and efficient claims processing.

**Key deliverables:**
- Contract documentation management
- Premium and claims bordereaux
- Statement reconciliation
- Regulatory reporting support

---

### 12. Run-off Management

For clients with legacy portfolios or discontinued lines of business, Guardian Re offers professional run-off management services. We ensure orderly administration and cost-effective resolution of historical exposures.

**Key deliverables:**
- Legacy portfolio assessment
- Run-off strategy development
- Claims management and commutation
- Portfolio transfer facilitation

---

## Our Approach

Guardian Re combines technical expertise with a client-centric approach. We invest time in understanding each client's business, risk appetite, and strategic objectives before recommending solutions. Our advice is independent, objective, and aligned with client interests.
