## About Guardian Reinsurance Brokers

Guardian Reinsurance Brokers (Z) Limited is Zambia's first and only dedicated reinsurance broking firm. Established in January 2006 and licensed by the Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA) in September of that year, we have built a reputation for professionalism, integrity, and technical excellence across the African insurance market.

---

### Our Heritage

For nearly two decades, Guardian Re has served as a trusted intermediary between insurers and the global reinsurance market. Our founding principle remains unchanged: to provide world-class, ethical reinsurance services that enable our clients to manage risk effectively while supporting the sustainable growth of their businesses.

As an endorsed broking partner of **Minerva Re**, we combine deep local market knowledge with access to international reinsurance capacity, expertise, and resources.

### Market Position

Guardian Re occupies a unique position in the Zambian market as the country's pioneering reinsurance broker. Our role extends beyond transactional placement to encompass advisory services, technical support, and strategic risk management guidance.

We serve insurance companies across multiple lines of business, including general insurance, life and health, and specialist risks. Our client relationships are built on trust, transparency, and a shared commitment to professional excellence.

---

### Regulatory Compliance

Guardian Re operates in full compliance with all applicable regulations issued by the Pensions and Insurance Authority. We maintain robust governance structures, adhere to prescribed conduct standards, and submit to regular regulatory oversight.

Our commitment to compliance reflects our broader responsibility to uphold the integrity of the Zambian insurance sector.
